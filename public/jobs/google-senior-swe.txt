# Senior Software Engineer - Google Cloud Platform

## About the role
As a Senior Software Engineer at Google Cloud Platform, you'll be at the forefront of cloud computing innovation, developing scalable solutions that power businesses worldwide. You'll work on complex distributed systems and help shape the future of cloud infrastructure.

## Location
Mountain View, CA

## What you'll do
- Design and implement highly scalable and reliable cloud services
- Lead technical projects and mentor junior engineers
- Collaborate with product and platform teams to define technical strategy
- Write and review high-quality code in languages like Go, Java, and Python
- Build and maintain critical infrastructure components
- Participate in the full software development lifecycle

## Qualifications
### Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent practical experience
- 8+ years of software development experience
- Strong expertise in distributed systems and cloud computing
- Proficiency in one or more programming languages (Go, Java, Python, C++)
- Experience with system design and architecture

### Preferred qualifications
- Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science
- Experience with cloud platforms (GCP, AWS, Azure)
- Contributions to open source projects
- Experience with microservices architecture
- Strong understanding of security best practices
- Track record of leading successful technical projects

## Skills & Competencies
- Expert-level programming and software architecture skills
- Strong problem-solving and analytical abilities
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
- Experience with agile development methodologies
- Knowledge of DevOps practices and tools
- Ability to mentor and lead technical teams

## What we offer
- Competitive salary and equity package
- Comprehensive health benefits
- Flexible work arrangements
- Learning and development opportunities
- 20% time to work on innovative projects
- Global impact and scale
